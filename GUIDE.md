# How to Use Trackers Hub

## Setup
Download the repository. Open `index.html` in any browser — Chrome or Edge recommended. No install, no internet needed after first load. Everything saves automatically to your browser's localStorage.

---

## Navigation
Every app has two controls in its nav bar:
- **Switch App** — jump to any of the 4 tools or back to the hub
- **⌘K / Search** — type anything ("attendance", "library", "timer") and press Enter to jump directly to that section

---

## Study Schedule Pro

**First time:**
- Go to Settings → set your daily study goal and add your subjects

**Daily:**
- Top bar shows your current class with a live countdown to the next one
- Click a subject tag on any todo to start its stopwatch — click again to pause, Stop to save
- Go to Attendance → tap Present or Absent for each class
- Add exams in Settings — hub card shows the countdown automatically

**Lobdell Timer:**
- Search "timer" to open it
- Runs 30 min study → 12 min review → 5 min break automatically
- Minimize it and keep using the app while it runs

---

## PROC//HUB

**First time:**
- Dashboard → set your weekly content budget in hours
- Library → Add Series → fill in name, type, total episodes

**Daily:**
- Open a series → tap an episode to mark it watched — time logs against your budget automatically
- Mood Queue → pick your mood → it filters your library to match
- Process Queue → add tasks for when you want to be productive instead
- Graveyard → move dropped series here to clean up your library

---

## Zen Garden

**Daily:**
- Open the app once a day → click today's tile to mark it done
- Optional journal note appears after clicking
- Missing a day shows a warning before resetting — no accidental streak breaks

**Milestones:**
- 7 days → One Week
- 30 days → Deep Roots
- 100 days → Elite Streak
- 365 days → Zen Master

---

## ORV Tracker

- Switch between Novel and Manhwa mode at the top
- Click a volume to mark it complete
- Read the Story So Far recap inside each volume before continuing
- Progress syncs to the hub dashboard automatically

---

## Hub Dashboard

| Card | Shows |
|------|-------|
| Study | Study % today, attendance rate, next exam countdown |
| PROC | Budget used this week, series count, tasks done |
| Zen | Current streak, this month's count, total days |
| ORV | Chapters read, volumes done out of 58 |

Updates every 3 seconds automatically.

---

## Your Data
- Saves in browser localStorage — no account needed
- Clearing browser cache will erase everything
- Data doesn't sync across devices or browsers
